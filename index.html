Slidor.

Those of us who are not hippies need money to live. And what better way to obtain it than to get people to give it to you ? This is what Slidor is (mainly) about: creating breathtaking presentations in Microsoft Powerpoint to impress investors.

This website was coded only by yours truly (at the time) but each page describing a production was done by a different designer. It involved ~brainstorming~ fighting a lot over what was possible and what was a good, possible idea with 7 people in a row... Print designers are great and make amazing assets but they are NOT easy on the developers for the web.
But we almost won an Awwward for it. https://www.awwwards.com/sites/slidor-1
